A paraplegic Marine, Jake Sully, takes his deceased twin brother's place in a mission on the alien moon Pandora. Using a genetically engineered body called an "avatar," he is tasked with infiltrating the native Na'vi people to convince them to move so humans can mine a valuable mineral. As he learns their ways and falls for a Na'vi woman, he becomes torn between his orders and his growing loyalty to the Na'vi, forcing him to choose sides in a conflict that could decide the fate of Pandora.
